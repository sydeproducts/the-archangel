ARCHANGEL INTEGRATED SYSTEM OF PROTECTION

Next Generation Firewall offers a full protection to any kind of devices and to data thanks to its exceptional feature

Each device contains 4 servers all of them created and manufactured by PT SYDECO: ARCHANGEL© Firewall Next Generation, SST© (Secure System of Transmission), VPN, and SydeCloud©.

ARCHANGEL© Next Generation is equipped with a Triple Firewalls Protection, 3 INTELLIGENT AGENTS, OPERATING SYSTEM ARCHANOS MICHAEL 18.2, 1 BRAIN, HONEY POT, DYNAMIC ROUTER, IDS, IPS, NAT SUPPORT, LOG ATTACKER, 2 SWITCHS, and INTERFACE.

VPN SERVER protected by ARCHANGEL and SST, with as many SSH TUNNELS as the users that the series are protecting.

SST© (Secure System of Transmission) is the QUANTUM SAFE encryption method created by PT SYDECO that is not using any key.
